4. Color Philosophy
Neutral-First Palette

Most of the UI lives in:

Whites

Soft grays

Transparent layers

Primary color is accentual, not dominant.

Rule

If everything is colored, nothing is important.

Accent Usage

Primary color is used for:

CTAs

Key metrics

Active state indicators

Small labels / chips

Never:

Full-page fills

Large backgrounds (except CTA blocks)

Gradients

Used sparingly, mainly for:

Hero text emphasis

Background atmospherics

Not for:

Buttons

Cards

Data surfaces

5. Card & Surface Language
Card Types

Granto uses three surface styles:

Neumorphic Cards

Soft shadow

Elevated but calm

Primary dashboard elements

Glass Panels

Backdrop blur

Semi-transparent

Secondary / contextual content

Solid Cards

Used for CTAs or highlights

High contrast, minimal detail

Rule

Choose card style based on importance, not aesthetics.

Borders & Shadows

Borders are subtle and often white or light gray

Shadows are:

Large

Diffuse

Low opacity

No sharp or dark shadows.

6. Data Visualization Language

Numbers first. Explanations second.
